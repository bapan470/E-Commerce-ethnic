WHAT CHANGED
============
This version replaces the placeholder/sample product with your REAL
products, pulled live from Supabase using the same helper your
sitemap.ts already uses (fetchProductsServer, from lib/products-api-server.ts).

No product data is hardcoded anymore — as soon as you add/edit/remove a
product in Supabase, the feed updates automatically.

HOW TO INSTALL
===============
1. In your repo, replace the existing file at:
   app/rss.xml/route.ts
   with the route.ts included in this zip (same path).

2. Commit and push to GitHub. Vercel will auto-redeploy.

3. Once deployed, open:
   https://www.aruhihandlooms.com/rss.xml

   You should see real products (sarees, lehenga, kurti, etc.) instead
   of "Sample Ethnic Kurta".

4. Validate the feed:
   https://www.w3.org/feed/validate.php

5. Paste the feed URL into Pinterest:
   Settings > Import content > RSS Feed URL
   https://www.aruhihandlooms.com/rss.xml
   Click Save.

NOTES
=====
- Only products where images[0] exists will show a picture in the feed.
  Make sure every product in Supabase has at least one image.
- Price is pulled directly from the "price" column (INR, whole rupees).
- Out-of-stock products still appear in the feed but marked
  "out of stock" — Pinterest may not show pricing/buy options for those.
