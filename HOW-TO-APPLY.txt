Admin ke "Products" (Catalog) tab me vendor listing progress dikhana
=====================================================================

Kya add hua
------------
Tumhare code me pehle se ek "Vendor Submissions" tab tha jo pending
products dikhata tha, lekin wo "Catalog" tab se alag jagah tha — isliye
admin ko yaad rakhna padta tha ki wahan jaake check karo. Ab "Catalog"
tab (jahan tumhara screenshot me "Products" list dikh rahi thi) ke
top pe ek amber-color banner add kiya hai jo turant dikhata hai:

  - Kitne vendor listings abhi live nahi hain
  - Har ek ka thumbnail, naam, vendor ka business name, category
  - Status badge — vendor apne dashboard me jo "⏳ Processing…" dekhta
    hai, wahi wording yahan bhi
  - "Review in Vendor Submissions →" button jo seedha us tab pe le jaata
    hai agar detail me dekhna ho

Jab tak koi listing "Processing…" (pending_review) me hai, banner har
12 second me apne aap refresh hota hai — jaise hi AI process complete
hoke product live hota hai, wo banner se apne aap hat jaata hai.
